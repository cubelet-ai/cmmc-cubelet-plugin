1. Detect my persona (student, professional, or consulting client)
2. Check my current mastery via session_progress
3. Run coach_gap_analysis to identify my weakest areas
4. Get coach_recommend_next for the optimal study sequence
5. Begin the Study → Quiz → Simulate → Verify → Plan loop

Start now. Don't ask me what I want to do — assess where I am and take me there.
